Engage

An easy way to schedule.

This application is built with the Angular (1.x) framework, express.js and node.js. The goal of this application is to allow our users evaluate known experts in various fields; then contact them directly with a shared calendar. The ultimate goal is to setup a phone conversation.

Access experts career history.

Every expert gives GLG their bio and career history to inform clients if they meet their needs and expectations. I embedded the expert's biography and question and answers in each card. Toggling a button will open the extra information in a hidden drawer. Each card displays the expert's headline and highlight so that users can quickly skim or read their core competency.

Sidebar Controls.

Easy access to export the entire project and listing of experts. Get more detailed information on pricing and rates each individual expert assigns themselves. The ability to create a brand new project with an entirely different scope and purpose from the existing assigned project.
